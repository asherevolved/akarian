# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Parents (and grandparents) of children aged 2–6, primarily in Whitefield, Bengaluru. Affluent, education-conscious, research-minded. They arrive on a phone or laptop, often after a referral, evaluating whether AKARIAN is a credible, premium choice for their child's earliest years. The job to be done: understand what EISE™ (Early Immersive Sensory Engagement) is, trust the science behind it, feel it is a gift rather than a remedy, and reach out to enquire/enrol.

## Product Purpose

A marketing landing page for AKARIAN®, "The Sensory Haven" — a structured, home-based sensory nourishment programme for children aged 2–6, built on the EISE™ framework across eight sensory pillars. The page exists to communicate the philosophy, establish scientific credibility, convey premium warmth, and convert interested parents into enquiries. Success = a parent leaves trusting AKARIAN and taking the contact step.

## Brand Personality

Three words: **Warm · Joyful · Nurturing.** Voice is human and reassuring, never institutional. It honours childhood as a gift to nourish, not a problem to fix. Scientific credibility is present but worn lightly — warmth leads, expertise supports.

> **Tension to resolve (design note):** the current copy leans *reverent / gravitas* ("a gift to be nourished," "absolutely and without compromise") which is more solemn than the chosen Warm/Joyful/Nurturing direction. Future copy and motion work should soften toward warmth and gentle joy without losing credibility. See [[design-warmth-tension]].

## Anti-references

- **Corporate SaaS** — no cards-everywhere grids, gradient hero, eyebrow-on-every-section template, hero-metric blocks.
- **Loud / salesy** — no discount banners, countdown timers, hype copy, or aggressive CTAs.
- **Clinical / medical** — not sterile, white-coat, or therapy-center. AKARIAN is proactive nourishment, not remedial treatment.

(Note: a warm, delightful, childhood-appropriate register IS welcome — generic-daycare brightness was *not* listed as an anti-reference. Playfulness is on the table; childishness is not.)

## Design Principles

1. **A gift, not a remedy.** Every section should feel like an offering of nourishment, never a fix for a deficit. Frame proactively.
2. **Warmth carries credibility.** Lead with human warmth; let the science support rather than dominate. Trust comes from care plus rigour, not jargon.
3. **Earned calm, not empty space.** Premium restraint and editorial rhythm — but warmth and joy must come through, not sterile minimalism.
4. **Craft is the proof.** For a programme about sensory richness and attention to detail, the page's own craft (motion, type, texture) is evidence of the brand's values. Practice what you preach.
5. **Honour the child.** Copy and imagery honour who the child already is. No shaping, no correcting — only nourishing.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target **WCAG 2.1 AA**. The codebase already implements: skip-to-content link, visible `:focus-visible` rings, full `prefers-reduced-motion` fallbacks, semantic text-color roles, and balanced/pretty text-wrap. Maintain ≥4.5:1 body contrast against ivory/sandstone backgrounds (watch sage-on-ivory body text), keep large display text ≥3:1, and ensure every GSAP/Lenis effect has a reduced-motion path. Parents may browse one-handed on phones in low-attention contexts — touch targets and legibility at small sizes matter.
